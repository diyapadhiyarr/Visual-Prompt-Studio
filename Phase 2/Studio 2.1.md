Medium A:Create a painting that uses watercolor that emphasizes color, contrast, texture, and movement. The composition should feature a peaceful lake at sunset with mountains in the background and a small wooden boat floating on the water. The watercolor should show soft gradients in the sky, watery bleeding edges where colors blend together, and transparent layers to create depth and harmony. The movement of the water should be suggested through loose brush strokes and flowing shapes, creating a calm and unified scene.

Medium B:Create a linocut print that emphasize lines, contrast, shape, and unity. The composition should feature a peaceful lake at sunset with mountains in the background and a small wooden boat floating on the water.The image should focus on strong black and white contrast, with bold carved lines representing the water movement and mountain textures. The boat and sunset should stand out through simplified shapes and dramatic contrast, creating a striking and graphic composition.
